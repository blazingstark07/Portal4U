About Us

Portal4U is a platform designed to ease the communication between the government and the citizens. Users can report various issues in their societies which will be prioritized based on how many similar issues were submitted in the same location. They will also receive regular updates on all government news and notices, along with guidelines for different government formalities and procedures.
