Step-by-Step Guide to your Documents

A complete guide to obatin important documents like Aadhar, PAN card and Voter ID.

Aadhar

Aadhaar is a verifiable 12-digit identification number issued by UIDAI to the resident of India for free of cost

Voter ID

A Voter ID Card, also known as the Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC) is a photo identity card that is issued by the Election Commission of India

PAN Card

A permanent account number (PAN) is a ten-character alphanumeric identifier, issued in the form of a laminated "PAN card"
